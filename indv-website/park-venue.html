Park Venue

Our park venue is perfect for a fun and exciting day in the sun. This venue has beautifully carved statues for your pleasure of all kinds and provides a beautifully made wooden gazebo for all to gather under. The gazebo can accommodate up to 50 people comfortably. Our entertainment at this barbeque style location include our awe-inspiring magicians, ear pleasing musicians, and world class singers. Our catering service for this site include our all-American brats and burgers, our flavor infused chicken wings, and our smoky barbeque rack of ribs.
